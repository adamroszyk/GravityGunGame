Enjoy Playing GravityGun Game!

To steer the player please use WSAD or Arrows.
To aim the gun move mouse up and down, and to shoot please press and hold Left mouse button, untill ball will be loaded and Release the LMB to Shoot the ball.

There're couple levels in the game with increasing level of difficulty, on the level 3rd you can find Two types of oponents- blocking and following the player.

Enjoy !
